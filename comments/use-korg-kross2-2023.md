Thanks!

Arturia MiniLab midi out -> Kross 2 midi in. But, before that I pre-configured the knobs and faders of Arturia MiniLab and mapping it to CC number of Kross 2.

Kross 2 audio out -> audio in of my audio interface. This is if I only need to record the sound, not for controlling any VSTs. If I want to control VSTs, I use its USB.
